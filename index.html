Handshake

Transmit your kindness or lack thereof using handshakes!

Controls

  • Click to start the game (fullscreen)
  • W - reach out (left click or tap on your arm works as well)
  • S - shake hands, reaches out if needed
  • D - next hand, pulls back if needed
  • P - punch (shake phone works too)
  • H - PFUI!, M - to heal

Requirements

  • Android + Chrome does not work due to a bug!
  • Chrome or Firefox (Desktop) or Android + Firefox
